Unleashed Firmware ships with a curated set of community apps and makes it straightforward to install more. This page explains what each build variant includes, where to find additional apps, and how to install them.

## Build variants and included apps

When you download an Unleashed Firmware release, the filename suffix tells you which app pack is included:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="e — Extra Pack" icon="boxes-stacked">
    The recommended build for most users. Includes the full **Extra Pack** of community apps on top of the base firmware apps. Choose this if you want the widest selection of pre-installed plugins and games.
  </Card>
  <Card title="(no suffix) — Base Pack" icon="box">
    Includes a curated **Base Pack** of community apps. Smaller than the Extra Pack but still includes the most commonly used plugins.
  </Card>
  <Card title="c — Minimal" icon="minimize">
    A minimal build with no extra community apps pre-installed. Choose this if you prefer to install apps manually or need the smallest possible firmware image.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  The `e` (Extra Pack) build is the recommended choice for new users who want the most functionality out of the box.
</Tip>

## Where to find apps

### Extra Pack and Base Pack

The full list of apps included in each pack, along with source code and release downloads, is maintained at [xMasterX/all-the-plugins](https://github.com/xMasterX/all-the-plugins/tree/dev) on GitHub. Pre-built releases are available at the [latest release page](https://github.com/xMasterX/all-the-plugins/releases/latest).

### Official Flipper Apps Catalog

The official Flipper Zero Apps Catalog at [lab.flipper.net/apps](https://lab.flipper.net/apps) lists apps reviewed and published by Flipper Devices. You can also browse and install from the catalog directly inside the Flipper mobile app.

## How to install apps

Apps are packaged as `.fap` files (Flipper Application Packages) and run directly from the microSD card without reflashing the firmware.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Via qFlipper">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect your Flipper">
        Connect your Flipper Zero to your computer via USB and open qFlipper.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the file manager">
        In qFlipper, navigate to the **File Manager** tab.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy the .fap file">
        Navigate to `SD Card/apps/` (or the appropriate subdirectory for the app category) and drag the `.fap` file into the folder.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch the app">
        On the Flipper, open the **Apps** menu and find your newly installed app in the matching category.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Via mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Apps Catalog">
        In the Flipper mobile app, tap the **Apps** tab to open the catalog.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find and install">
        Browse or search for an app, then tap **Install**. The app is sent to your Flipper over Bluetooth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch the app">
        The app appears in the **Apps** menu on your Flipper, ready to use.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Built-in app tutorials

Several apps included in Unleashed Firmware have dedicated usage guides.

### Barcode Generator

The Barcode Generator app (by [McAzzaMan](https://github.com/McAzzaMan/flipperzero-firmware/tree/UPC-A_Barcode_Generator/applications/barcode_generator)) generates UPC-A barcodes directly on the Flipper Zero display.

**Controls:**

- Press the **center** button to toggle edit mode.
- In edit mode, use **Left** and **Right** to select the digit to change.
- Use **Up** and **Down** to adjust the selected digit's value.

<Note>
  The Barcode Generator currently supports UPC-A format only. Additional barcode types are planned for future releases.
</Note>

### Multi Converter

The Multi Converter app (by [theisolinearchip](https://github.com/theisolinearchip/flipperzero_stuff/tree/main/applications/multi_converter)) converts values between units across multiple categories.

**Supported conversions:**

- Decimal / Hexadecimal / Binary
- Celsius / Fahrenheit / Kelvin
- Kilometers / Meters / Centimeters / Miles / Feet / Inches
- Degree / Radian

**Controls:**

- The on-screen keyboard supports digits `0`–`F` (keys are disabled when not valid for the selected unit).
- Long press **0** to toggle a negative value.
- Long press **1** to insert a decimal point (where allowed).
- Press **`<`** to delete the last character.
- Press **`#`** to enter Unit Select Mode.

**Unit Select Mode:**

- Use **Left** and **Right** to switch between the origin unit and the destination unit.
- Press **OK** to confirm and return to display mode.
- Press **Back** to cancel without changing units.
