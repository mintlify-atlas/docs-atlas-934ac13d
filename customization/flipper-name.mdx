---
title: "Flipper name"
description: "Change or reset the Flipper Zero device name shown in device info, the passport screen, and over Bluetooth."
---

Unleashed Firmware lets you set a custom name for your Flipper Zero. The name is stored on the microSD card and persists across firmware updates.

## Change the device name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Desktop settings">
    On your Flipper Zero, navigate to **Settings → Desktop → Change Flipper Name**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a new name">
    Use the on-screen keyboard to type your new name, then press **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for reboot">
    You will see a `Name is set!` confirmation. When you exit Settings, the Flipper automatically reboots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the name">
    After rebooting, your new name appears in **Device Info** and on the right-side **Passport** screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reset the name to default

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Desktop settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Desktop → Change Flipper Name**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save without entering a name">
    Leave the name field empty and press **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for reboot">
    Exit Settings. The Flipper reboots and the name reverts to its original factory default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reconnect Bluetooth after a name change

Changing the device name affects how the Flipper advertises itself over Bluetooth. Your phone will no longer recognize the previous pairing.

<Note>
  After renaming your Flipper, you must forget the old pairing on both your phone and the Flipper itself before re-pairing.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Forget the device in the mobile app">
    In the Flipper mobile app, select **Forget Flipper**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the pairing from your phone">
    Open your phone's **Settings → Bluetooth**, find the Flipper in the device list, open its options, and select **Forget device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Forget devices on the Flipper">
    On the Flipper, go to **Settings → Bluetooth → Forget all devices** and confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-pair">
    Make sure Bluetooth is **ON** on the Flipper, then open the mobile app and pair again from scratch.
  </Step>
</Steps>
